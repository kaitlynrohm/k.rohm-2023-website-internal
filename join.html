How to join the club

In the form below input your first name, last name and physical address where requested. In the first choose file option add in proof of your address. This could be a copy of your power bill. In the second choose file option put proof of ID. A copy of your drivers licence or passport.

Your membership will be confirmed by email. Additional information about the Club and accommodation (membership letter) will be sent by regular mail.

If you wish to contact us please email maungaclub@maungaclub.maungaclub or click the button below!
